Name Enua Ambystoma Hespera
Pronouns she/her
Age 27 (human years)
Species Erisian Alien
Height 195cm
Role Celebrity (Adult Film Star) + Corruptor
A sly and cunning alien, Enua is one of the top dogs illegally overseeing and manipulating the operations of Demise Corp; a mega corporation that owns and operates a mass number of companies, media outlets and programs all around the solar system.

The corporation's existence has been a cause for many hardships for Enua growing up. As a child she lost her whole family to a disease outbreak caused by its biochemical experiments in which treatment was too costly for any of them to afford. She barely managed to scrap by, and later on found a niche in doing sex work. It worked out so well that she quickly became one of the richest and most well-known aliens on Eris, and she now uses her wealth and reputation to take control of Demise Corp and rule the solar system.

Enua constantly tries to sabotage Sketchy's operations because she's aware of the threat they bring to her plans.
